Robot

Discover "The Autonomous Explorer," a distinctive 3D robot model I crafted using Blender and Substance Painter, without any external references. This model exemplifies a seamless blend of industrial design and intricate detailing. Meticulously designed, from its articulate joints to the detailed optic sensors, this robot is a testament to my ability to create from pure imagination. It stands as a striking example of my skills in 3D modeling and texturing, showcasing the power of creativity unleashed by advanced digital tools such as blender and substance painter.
